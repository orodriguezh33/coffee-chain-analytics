# Usage Playbooks

## Store Manager (daily)
1. Open Waste & Inventory dashboard before opening.
2. Check `Stockout HIGH` items and place urgent orders if needed.
3. Check top waste ingredients and brief the team.
4. Open Labor dashboard and validate staffing against peak hours.

## Operations Director (weekly)
1. Open Executive Summary for MoM trend and store comparison.
2. Open Branch & Product dashboard for branch scorecard and waste pressure.
3. Review financial deep dive for margin mix and category profitability.
4. Define weekly actions (staffing, promotions, process fixes).
