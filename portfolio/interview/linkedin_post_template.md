# LinkedIn Post Template

## Hook
I built the analytics system I wish I had when working coffee retail operations.

## Problem
Store teams often decide staffing, ordering, and promotions without integrated data. POS alone cannot explain margin, waste, or labor efficiency.

## Solution
I built an end-to-end platform using S3 + Athena + dbt + Airflow, with a BI layer focused on five decision dashboards. The key differentiator is waste tracking from cross-source joins (sales + recipe BOM + physical inventory).

## Outcome
Daily decisions become measurable: margin visibility, stockout risk before opening, labor efficiency by store and day.

## Stack
S3 | Athena | dbt | Airflow | Power BI
